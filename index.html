About Me

Some of my hobbies are cooking, collecting watches and FunkoPop figures. Currently, I have around 13 watches and upwards of 80+ FunkoPop figures. I also like to watch cooking videos and trying to recreate the recipes. I recently bought a cookbook so I can have a base for some recipes and changing it up to how I like things and hopefully will be able to develop some recipes of my own as I learn more.
